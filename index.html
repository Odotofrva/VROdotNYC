How to use VR OdotNYC

To preview the realm on-screen, place your phone into your cardboard viewer, then enter VR mode to explore the selected low-poly scene.

1

Select a realm and preview it by dragging to rotate and pinching to zoom.

2

Set screen brightness high, mute extra tabs, and rotate your phone to landscape before placing it into your cardboard viewer.

3

Tap Enter VR. If your browser supports immersive mode, use the headset icon inside the scene for a more native VR handoff.

4

Look at the left and right hotspot circles to switch worlds. The center ring acts as a gaze cursor.
